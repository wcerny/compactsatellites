Data Products

Summary Tables

We make available key catalogs and tables associated with our analysis in Paper I in a Zenodo repository. These tables summarize our main results and are intended for quick reference.

Individual Member Catalogs

Users of products derived from DEIMOS spectroscopy (applicable to 12 of the 19 UFCSs) can access detailed catalogs listing individual members. Please ensure to cite Geha et al. (2026) when using these datasets.

Citing Data Products

When utilizing any of these tables or catalogs in your work, please reference both Paper I and Geha et al. (2026) as appropriate. This ensures proper attribution for the data and methods used in your analysis.
